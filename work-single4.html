Tandem Frame

Individual Projec for Finite Element Analysis

This report aims to present the design and ideation process of a 3D modelled tandem bicycle frame, while considering specific requirements provided. The primary goal is to design a lightweight frame with natural frequencies larger than 30Hz to avoid discomfort due to whole-body vibrations, and with an effective life of at least 10 years, equivalent to one million loading cycles. Through FEA analysis of the prototyped model, this report examines the frame's behaviour under different loading conditions and evaluates its performance against the given criteria. By providing an in-depth analysis of the design process and the use of FEA methods, this report aims to contribute to the development of tandem bicycle frames that offer optimal performance and safety for riders.
