    Smartwatch for deaf people

    Individual Project for Industrial Design Engineering

    This SmartWatch incorporates vibrating alerts for notifications and environmental awareness,ensuring users stay informed. Visual notifications are displayed on the watch face for easy visibility.It also includes sign language recognition and translation technology, facilitating communication.An emergency alert system is integrated to quickly contact emergency contacts in case ofunexpected situations. The watch promotes mental and physical health with features like heart ratemonitoring, activity tracking, and sleep tracking.
