    Bio-Inspired Hexa-Pedal Robot

    Individual Project for Physical Computing

    RHex imitates the movement of insects and small animals to navigate challenging terrain. Its six legs and its simple control system mimics neutral circuits found in insects. RHex is adaptable and ideal for search and rescue, reconnaissance, and environmental monitoring, and could pave the way for more efficient robotic systems in the future.
