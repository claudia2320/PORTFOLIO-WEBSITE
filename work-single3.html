    Vehicle Design & Aerodynamics

    Individual Project for Thermodynamics

    This two-door sports car concept is inspired by the McLaren 570S. The main purpose of this car is to be differentiated from other poor handling and sub-standard boot space supercars which are not universally excellent, despite their exorbitant cost. Luxury and high-performance are two of the main features of this model, however, its main objective is to offer a possibility of getting a reduction in fuel consumption and emission levels by its outside design features.
